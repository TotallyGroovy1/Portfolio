About Enoch.Photography:

I'm a photographer specializing in nature, landscape, and wildlife photography.

I started getting interested in photography in late 2019 when my sister took a course in videography and got a new camera. Eventually, I started to use that camera and slowly learned the basics of photography. I would constantly bring my camera everywhere, shooting as many things as I could in manual mode despite the reccomendations from my dad to learn to shoot in auto first. This would build my knowledge of camera technique and skills. Eventually I took a course in photography, learning more about composition and story telling.
